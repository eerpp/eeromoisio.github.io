Hydraulic Press Build

Hydraulic horseshoe bending machine designed to allow horseman to shape horseshoes without the use of hammer or heating the metal. I was part of designing and choosing hydraulic components, making the CAD model, as well as the physical building of the machine, mainly manufacturing the fram out of square profile pipe and welding the whole contraption together. The project was succesful and was able to shape horseshoes with ease. The project was taken into use by an actual horseman, adding to his mobile arsenal of horseshoe shaping tool arsenal.

RC drifting gyro

An arduino based RC drifting gyroscope to produce realistic countersteer. The system uses MPU6050 IMU to read the yaw rate of the RC car, and uses this to command the steering servo to perform countersteering to replicate the actions of real cars. The project has turned out very good, being able to compete with commercial products in terms of performance. The operating logic of the countersteer is based on PD control of yaw rate.

RC car windtunnel

A windtunnel built with a four-point scale to measure the weight and effect of downforce for each wheel. The windtunnel was powered by an automotive cooling fan, achieving wind speeds of around 10 m/s. An arduino in combination with four strain gauges was used to measure the individual wheels, as well as perform PWM control over a mosfet in order to have control of windspeeds. The obtained data clearly showed the development of downforce for tested RC cars. Surprisingly the result was that a traditional vehicle shaped RC car actually ended up creating lift instead of downforce. The addition of a wind reduced the amount of lift created. The formula style car showcased in the photo was the only tested RC car to create downforce instead of lift.

Drive by Wire kit

A ROS2 based system that allows for operation of vehicle wheel and pedals with a digital input. The input used for the video is a gamepad controller. Yolo based machine vision control was also perfromed. The system achieved resonable control over a Saab 900 vehicle in slow speed operation. Slowness of wheel and pedal actuators limits current performance. This drive by wire kit was part of the Mechatronics project course of 2025. A memorable visit to Tallin to attend the modern materials & manufactoring 2025 conference where we showcased the results of the project was part of the experience.
